Welcome to Enbridge Énov We are delighted to have you with us! At Enbridge Énov, we are committed to providing you with quality appliances as well as an unparalleled customer experience! Here are some useful and inspiring information for you.

Who we are

Enbridge Énov stands out for its commitment to providing energy-efficient solutions that meet the growing needs of our society. As a new brand created by Enbridge Gaz Québec, an energy distributor serving the Outaouais region, Enbridge Énov benefits from the expertise and know-how of a well-established company with 65 years of experience. With a customer satisfaction rate of 92% and over 45,000 rental appliances, this experience is a major asset in meeting consumer expectations.

How to reach us

For any questions regarding your invoice, our customer service representatives are available Monday to Friday, from 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM. For any technical questions or assistance, our support service is available 24/7. To contact us, call 1-844-550-ENOV or email us at enov@enbridge.com . You can also visit our website at enbridgeenov.com .

Your invoice

Each month, we will prepare your statement of account which will be accessible via our Customer Portal. Convenient and secure, the Customer Portal allows you to quickly access your invoices. No action is required on your part. We have already completed your registration for online billing. When your invoice is ready, you will receive an email notification.

How to pay your invoice

Various simple and secure payment methods are available to you. You can pay your invoice in the way that suits you best; join our automatic debit program via your bank account. This solution is accessible in your Customer Portal and can be suspended at any time. Alternatively, you can pay at an ATM, by phone, or online through your bank: you must select Enbridge Énov, as the supplier.
