Did you know?

Your meter must at all times be visible, and must never be covered by a tarp or any sort of structure. In the interest of safety, clear away any snow buildup.

If your meter is covered in ice, contact our Customer Portal as soon as possible.

Are you going to be away for a while?

If you’re going to be away for a short while (less than two weeks), Enbridge Gaz Québec recommends you lower the thermostat setting by 2 or 3 degrees.

Several appliances can be adapted while you’re away to optimize your heating or air conditioning. That’s the case, for instance, with standard natural gas water heater (connected to a chimney), which can be set a “vacation” mode. Power vent water heaters can simply be disconnected while you are away, and for additional safety, shut off the cold water supply to limit the risk of damage from leakage. For electric water heaters, switch off the power supply as well as the cold water supply. Save energy!
