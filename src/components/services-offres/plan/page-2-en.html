A few tips and trickss

to optimize your consumption

  1. Be in control with a smart thermostat. As your ally in your energy saving efforts, it adapts to yours habits preferences. You can control it remotely through you smartphone, as it responds to the temperature fluctuations in your home and integrates your routine. If you are eligible, when your purchase and install a smart thermostat, you will receive $100.
  2. Make the most of natural light: open your curtains on sunny days.
  3. Change your furnace filter! A clean filter helps deliver optimal performance. Change it every three months to maintain your system.
  4. Set your ceiling fan direction clockwise in winter to run your heating efficiently, and counter-clockwise in the summer to pull the hot air up. A simple trick to improve air circulation!
  5. Keep furnace intake and exhaust pipes clear of obstructions (*if you have a forced air system).
